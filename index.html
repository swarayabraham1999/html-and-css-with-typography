The Light of Faith

In darkness deep, a spark ignites,
A whisper soft in endless nights.
Through trials fierce, it burns so bright,
Guiding hearts to truth and light.

Not wealth nor might, nor fleeting gain,
Can soothe the soul or ease its pain.
But faith, like stars in skies so vast,
Brings peace that ever holds steadfast.

So walk the path, though rough it seems,
With trust in Him, fulfill your dreams.
For every tear, a joy is near,
And faith shall cast away all fear.

The Climb to Success

Mountains rise, so steep, so high,
But wings of will can touch the sky.
With every fall, a lesson grows,
Through storm and strife, the strong one knows.

The road is long, the nights are cold,
Yet dreams shine bright like molten gold.
No easy way, no path so clear,
But step by step, you’ll persevere.

So rise again, despite the pain,
Through sweat and loss, through toil and gain.
For those who dare, who strive, who try,
Shall one day stand where eagles fly.

The Whispering Trees

The wind it hums, the leaves they dance,
In nature’s arms, a soft romance.
The river sings, the flowers bloom,
A painted world without a gloom.

The morning sun, so warm, so bright,
Awakens fields in golden light.
And when the dusk begins to call,
The stars like lanterns gently fall.

Oh, cherish this, the earth so grand,
A gift of life in nature’s hand.
For every tree and wave and breeze,
Are whispered songs of endless peace.

Unbreakable Soul

The storm may rage, the night may weep,
But strong are those who rise from deep.
For pain will pass, and wounds will heal,
Yet courage stays, like tempered steel.

No weight too vast, no chain too tight,
Can steal the will to stand and fight.
So though the world may try to break,
A heart of fire will never shake.

Stand tall, press on, let doubt decay,
For dawn will come and light the way.
And in the end, through scars you bear,
You'll find yourself beyond despair.
